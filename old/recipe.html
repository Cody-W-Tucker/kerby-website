RANCHERO NACHO DIP

1lb. Owen's country sausage (mild)
16 oz. Velveeta cheese
2 cans tomatoes & chilli greens

Fry off sausage; drain. Melt cheese. Partially drain tomatoes and chilli greens. Add to melted cheese. Add saussage. Serve warm on plain nachos. For milder version, use just one can tomatoes and chilli green.

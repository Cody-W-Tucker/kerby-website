THE HOMECOMING Item #61
When you are viewing The Homecoming you just get that warm fuzzy feeling. This heart warming timeless classic is considered to be Kerby's most popular painting. What a wonderful addition to any home, office or collection.

LONELY BUT FREE I'LL BE FOUND Item #62
This beautiful piece creates a special mood and feeling. There is a little bit of this painting in each of us. The subtleness in Lonely But Free I'll Be Found is moving beyond words. An excellent gift for anyone!

ONE DAY OLD Item #63
Horse lovers have a special connection to One Day Old. This mare and her newborn colt really stand for unity. Makes a nice grouping with Distant Thunder and Ranch Saddle Horses.

ONE SHORT Item #64
Those calves can be hard to spot and this little guy is hiding out. A real favorite with cattle lovers. You'll enjoy the beautiful scenery in One Short.

ROCKY MOUNTAIN HIGH Item #65
Kerby takes you right up to the High Country in this piece. The depth and gorgeous scenery you'll experience is unbelievable. You'll love the colors and peaceful tranquility in Rocky Mountain High

DISTANT THUNDER Item #66
Looks like a storm is moving in out on the Plains. Bob actually caught a picture of the thunder head coming in over the ranch one July. A favorite with horse lovers, Distant Thunder makes a nice grouping with One Day Old and Ranch Saddle Horses.

OH, TO BE A COWBOY Item #67
Taking care of livestock means working in all weather conditions. Lucky for the cow and calf the cowboy is heading 'em for the barn. You'll appreciate the artistic expression and painterly technique.

SOLITARY MAN Item #68
Winter on the open plains, a lone cowboy doing his job, and a windmill in the distance creates a peaceful, tranquil mood. This piece has been a popular gift for men.

PUNCHIN' COWS AT CROW CREEK Item #69
You'll love how Bob has the sunlight hitting the rider's horse and cattle in Punchin' Cows at Crow Creek. A subtle and powerful piece, it has been very popular and makes a nice set with One Short.

BREAKING CAMP Item #70
Wow! What a backdrop to this piece. We're in the high country here. A favorite among hunters, campers, hikers and outdoor enthusiasts.

RANCH SADDLE HORSES Item #71
Kerby uses the light to create highlights, shadows and depth. You'll enjoy the way Ranch Saddle Horses reads across a room. Makes a nice grouping with One Day Old and Distant Thunder.

SAGEBRUSH SPORT Item #72
Do you know a team roper, or rope yourself? This makes a fantastic gift. The ropers really stand out against the open plains and big sky. Get ready. The action is happening!
